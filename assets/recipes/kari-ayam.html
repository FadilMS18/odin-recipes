Chicken Curry

Curry is one of the typical types of cuisine that originated in India. This curry dish then spread to various parts of the world and was loved by many people, including Indonesia. Each country has their own curry variants. Curry dishes are generally synonymous with spicy, savory, and thick flavors with a variety of fillings. One of the most popular curry dishes is the special chicken curry recipe. Not only using chicken, the special chicken curry recipe also comes with diced potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • Chickens, cut into 12 pieces, clean - 1 head
  • Potatoes, cut into 4 parts - 4 pieces
  • Curry leaves - 2 stems
  • Thick coconut milk - 300 ml
  • Water - 200 ml
  • Cardamom - 3 grains
  • Lawang flower - 1 floret
  • Cloves - 2 pieces
  • Curry powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Powdered broth to taste - to taste
  • Cooking oil - 3 tbsp

FINE SEASONINGS:

  • Shallots - 10 grains
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Red pepper - 6 pieces
  • Ginger - 1 finger segment
  • Grilled turmeric - 2 finger
  • Coriander, roast - 1 tsp
  • Cumin, roasted - 1 tsp
  • Pecan, roasted - 3 grains

How to Make Chicke Curry:

  • Pre-boil the chicken by adding a pinch of salt, wait for it to boil. Don't forget to turn it aside.
  • Heat cooking oil, saut√© the spices until fragrant.
  • Add curry powder, curry leaves, as well as spices such as cinnamon, lawang flowers, cardamom and cloves, mix well until the curry leaves wither.
  • Put the potatoes, chicken and water, mix well and then cover the pan. Wait for the potatoes to be soft and the spices to soak into the chicken.
  • Add thick coconut milk, spike salt and chicken broth to taste. Mix well. Wait for it to boil while stirring constantly so that the coconut milk does not break. Correct the taste, if it is just right turn off the fire.
  • Coriander, cumin and turmeric must be burned so that the resulting flavor is more fragrant.
  • If the chicken is cut into small pieces, it does not need to be boiled first.
