Fried Rice/Nasi Goreng

This first recipe is oriental fried rice that is not given additional soy sauce. The color is yellowish white with a mixture of charsiun chicken, shrimp and also chicken eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 500 gr fluffed rice
  • 100 gr charsiu BBQ chicken
  • 100 gr peeled shrimp
  • 3 chicken eggs
  • 5 gr coarsely
  • Sliced leeks Chow fried rice seasoning:
  • 10 gr salt
  • 10 gr chicken
  • broth powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

How to Make Yang Chow Fried Rice:

  • Wash the shrimp and then cut it into pieces
  • Cut the roast chicken into pieces.
  • Heat the oil in a pan, put the shrimp and chicken.
  • Cook for a while until cooked. Drain.
  • Heat a little oil in a pan.
  • Put the eggs, stirring until they are clumped and cooked.
  • Put the rice, stirring until it decomposes.
  • Add shrimp and grilled chicken. Mix well.
  • Marinade with salt, powdered chicken broth and soy sauce.
  • Stir thoroughly and then remove from heat.
